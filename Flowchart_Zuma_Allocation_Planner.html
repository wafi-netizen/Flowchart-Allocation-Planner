2 Calculation Flow: Kapasitas ke Ideal Stock

START
↓
STEP 1
Ambil Data Kapasitas Store
Dari Master Data
Contoh: Store TP = 200 Box
↓
STEP 2
Hitung Traffic Profile
Dari Data Sales 12 Bulan

Formula: Tier Contribution Ratio

Tier_Ratio = (Sales_Tier / Total_Sales) × 100%

Contoh Store TP (Total 10,000 pair):
  • T1: 4,500 pair = 45%
  • T2: 2,500 pair = 25%
  • T3: 1,500 pair = 15%
  • T8: 1,000 pair = 10%
  • T4+T5: 500 pair = 5%
↓
STEP 3
Hitung Ideal Stock per Tier
Kapasitas × Tier Ratio

Formula: Ideal Stock

Ideal_Stock_Tier = Kapasitas × Tier_Contribution_Ratio

Contoh Store TP (Kapasitas 200 box):
  • Ideal T1: 45% × 200 = 90 box
  • Ideal T2: 25% × 200 = 50 box
  • Ideal T3: 15% × 200 = 30 box
  • Ideal T8: 10% × 200 = 20 box
  • Ideal T4+T5: 5% × 200 = 10 box
  • Total: 200 box = Kapasitas Store
↓
STEP 4
Ambil Data Actual Stock
Export dari Accurate
↓
STEP 5
Bandingkan Actual vs Ideal
Selisih = Actual - Ideal
↓
Selisih > 0
↓
SURPLUS
Actual > Ideal
Perlu Redistribusi
Selisih = 0
↓
OPTIMAL
Actual = Ideal
No Action
Selisih < 0
↓
DEFICIT
Actual < Ideal
Butuh Tambahan
↓
OUTPUT: Surplus/Deficit Report
